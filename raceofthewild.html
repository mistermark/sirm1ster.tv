Gentlemen's Race of the Wild

Introduction

On May 29th sirM1ster and TheRealSeiferoth are going head-to-head in a race, dubbed “The Gentlemen's Race of the Wild”. We're celebrating our 1-year Twitch anniversary in style by challenging each other how well we know our first game we've streamed.

The Distinguished Gentlemen on Twitch:

  • sirM1ster
  • TheRealSeiferoth

Goals

Goals of the race are to complete the following main targets. The order of which should be decided by each player. There’s no explicit order in which the targets need to be completed (aside from Plateau first, obviously).

  • Complete Great Plateau
    • 1 Tower
    • 4 Shrines
    • Collect paraglider
  • Divine Beasts
    • Vah Ruta
    • Vah Rudania
    • Vah Medoh
    • Vah Naboris
  • Collect Master Sword
    • A minimum of 24 shrines must be completed in order to acquire Master Sword legitimately
    • At least one Major Test of Strength Shrine must be completed
  • Collect Hylian Shield
  • Player has to defeat at least one of each of the following monsters:
    • White-maned Lynel
    • Black Hinox
    • Igneo Talus OR Frost Talus
    • Molduga
    • Guardian Stalker
  • Defeat Ganon with “classic equipment” (Master Sword & Hylian Shield) equipped

Explicit exceptions:

Below is a list of items that don’t explicitly require completion to complete the race:

  • Activate all towers
  • Activation of all 120 shrines
  • Recall all memories
  • Upgrade of Sheikah Slate
  • Korok Seeds/Inventory Expansion

General Rules

  • No Major Glitches can be used during the race, including:
    • Shrine/Wall clipping
    • Shield Clipping
    • Scope Clipping
    • Deliberate attempts to get Out of Bounds
    • Slate Storage
    • Flying Machine
    • Cutscene Storage
    • New Game+ style glitches
    • Early Master Sword (EMS)
    • Heart Duplication
    • Wrong Warp/Shrine Co-ordinate Warp (SCW)
    • Spawn Displacement
    • Damaging a boss during it’s cutscene (including Test of Strength shrines)
    • Collision Overload
    • Menu Overload
    • Deliberate steps to generate lag for “Super Launches” (including the use of VR mode)
      • Accidental Super Launches won’t be penalized since it can’t always be controlled
    • Anything else not explicitly agreed between participants beforehand
  • The following tricks are allowed:
    • Bomb Impact Launch (Windbomb)
    • Bullet Time Bounce
    • Shield jump
    • Fall Damage Cancel
    • Animation Cancel (e.g. jumping to shorten weapon attacks)
    • Stasis Launch
    • Whistle-Sprint/Throw-Sprint
    • Bow-spin
    • Ragdolling
    • Skipping Lost Woods via one of the allowed tricks IS permitted but the Korok Forest must be fully loaded as normal (see below regarding Master Sword).
  • If a player finds themselves accidentally out of bounds, they must do everything possible to get back in bounds as soon as possible.
  • All items must be collected from their original source
    • Orbs must come from shrines
    • Master Sword must be acquired via legit means from its pedestal, complete with cutscene triggers. UNLOADED FOREST NOT PERMITTED.
  • Timer starts from the moment player gains control of Link
  • Timer stops when the last arrow hits Beast Ganon’s eye (first frame of explosion animation)
  • Players may decide their own routes and/or use splits at their own discretion
  • Objectives may be completed in any order
  • Players must play in English on real Nintendo Switch hardware to eliminate timing discrepancies
