Gentlemen's Race of the Wild

Introduction

On May 29th sirM1ster and TheRealSeiferoth are going head-to-head in a race dubbed “The Gentlemen's Race of the Wild”. We're celebrating a couple of coincidences: we both started streaming on the same day AND we both played the same game on our first ever stream (before either of us even met each other). So we decided to celebrate our joint Twitch anniversary by challenging each other at the game that started it all... The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild.

The Distinguished Gentlemen on Twitch:

  • sirM1ster
  • TheRealSeiferoth

Goals

The primary criteria for this race are the same as MSD (Master Sword and Dungeons), but with some added twists for some extra challenge and fun:

  • Complete Great Plateau
    • 1 Tower
    • 4 Shrines
    • Collect paraglider
  • Divine Beasts
    • Vah Ruta
    • Vah Rudania
    • Vah Medoh
    • Vah Naboris
  • Collect Master Sword
    • A minimum of 24 shrines must be completed in order to acquire Master Sword legitimately
    • At least one Major Test of Strength Shrine must be completed
  • Collect Hylian Shield
  • Player has to defeat at least one of each of the following monsters:
    • White-maned Lynel
    • Black Hinox
    • Igneo Talus OR Frost Talus
    • Molduga
    • Guardian Stalker
  • Defeat Ganon with “classic equipment” (Master Sword & Hylian Shield) equipped

Exclusions:

The following objectives are not relevant, and therefore not required, to complete the race:

  • Activation of any towers beyond the one required on the plateau
  • Activation of remaining shrines beyond those chosen to collect the required number of orbs
  • Recalled Memories
  • Sheikah Slate Upgrades
  • Korok Seeds/Inventory Expansion

Rules

  • The following techniques/tricks/mechanics are permitted during the race:
    • Bomb Impact Launch (Windbomb)
    • Bullet Time Bounce
    • Shield jump
    • Fall Damage Cancel
    • Animation Cancel (e.g. jumping to shorten weapon attacks)
    • Stasis Launch
    • Whistle-Sprint/Throw-Sprint
    • Infinite Horse Stamina (IHS)
    • Bow-spin
    • Exploiting Ragdoll Status
    • Skipping Lost Woods is permitted using one of the permitted techniques BUT the Korok Forest must be fully loaded as normal (see below regarding Master Sword)
  • The following techniques/tricks/mechanics are NOT permitted:
    • Shrine/Wall clipping
    • Shield Clipping
    • Scope Clipping
    • Deliberate attempts to get Out of Bounds
      • If a player finds themselves accidentally out of bounds, they must do everything possible to get back in bounds as soon as possible
    • Slate Storage
    • Flying Machine
    • Cutscene Storage
    • New Game+ style glitches
    • Early Master Sword (EMS)
    • Heart Duplication
    • Wrong Warp/Shrine Co-ordinate Warp (SCW)
    • Spawn Displacement
    • Damaging a boss during it’s cutscene (including Test of Strength shrines)
    • Collision Overload
    • Menu Overload
    • Deliberate steps to generate lag for “Super Launches” (including the use of VR mode)
      • Accidental Super Launches won’t be penalized since it can’t always be controlled
    • Any other techniques/tricks/glitches/exploits/mechanics not explicitly agreed between participants beforehand
  • Amiibos are NOT permitted
  • All items must be collected from their original source
    • Orbs must come from shrines
    • Master Sword must be acquired via legit means from its pedestal, complete with cutscene triggers. UNLOADED FOREST NOT PERMITTED.

Rules

  • Players must play in English on real Nintendo Switch hardware to eliminate timing discrepancies
  • Timer starts from the moment player gains control of Link
  • Timer stops when the last arrow hits Beast Ganon’s eye (first frame of explosion animation)
  • Players may decide their own routes and/or use splits at their own discretion
  • Objectives may be completed in any order
  • Dying carries no penalty but the timer must continue to run as normal
  • Reloading a save is also permitted but the timer must continue to run as normal
  • Resetting/Restarting the run is NOT permitted
  • Except in case of emergency, breaks are limited to no more than one per hour and must be taken at the same time
    • When taking breaks, players should press Home (to ensure no game logic is processing whilst paused) and pause LiveSplit timers at the same time
    • When returning from breaks, players should press Home and unpause timers at the same time to resume
